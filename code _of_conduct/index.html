College Code of Conduct

The College Code of Conduct ensures a safe and respectful environment for students, faculty, and staff to thrive academically and socially.

  • Carry your identity card at all times on College Premises.
  • Maintain cleanliness within classrooms, laboratories, and campus areas.
  • Do not disrupt teaching, research, or administrative activities.
  • Mobile phones are prohibited on College Premises.
  • Handle College property carefully to avoid penalties or suspension.
  • Maintain at least 75% attendance; failure may result in exclusion from exams.

Consequences of Violations

Failure to comply with the Code of Conduct can lead to disciplinary actions including warnings, suspension, or expulsion from the College.
